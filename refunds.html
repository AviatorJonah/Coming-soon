Refund Policy for AviatorJonah, LLC

At AviatorJonah, we strive to provide high-quality products and services to our customers. Please review our refund policy below:

  1. All Sales Are Final

    Once a purchase is made, it is considered final. We do not offer refunds or exchanges.

  2. Case-by-Case Review

    While all sales are final, we understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. If you believe your situation warrants a refund, please reach out to us. Refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

  3. Contact Information

    To submit a refund request, please email us at refunds@avjco.org. Include your order number and a detailed explanation of your request.

  4. Response Time

    We aim to respond to all refund inquiries within 5 business days.
